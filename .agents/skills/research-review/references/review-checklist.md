# Research Review Checklist

## Scope
- Does the artifact still answer the approved question?
- Did the scope silently expand?

## Evidence
- Can every important claim be traced to evidence?
- Are conflicting sources preserved?
- Are weak sources labeled as weak?

## Benchmarks
- Are metrics comparable?
- Are dataset versions/splits aligned?
- Is evaluation setup compatible enough to compare?

## Reasoning
- Are interpretations distinguished from facts?
- Are limitations and alternative explanations surfaced?

## Process
- Were required approval gates respected?
- Does the artifact say what still needs user approval?
