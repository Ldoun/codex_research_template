# Verification Rubric

Use these verdicts consistently:

- **verified**: a direct check passed for the intended scope
- **partially verified**: some meaningful checks passed, but coverage is incomplete
- **unverified**: no reliable check has run yet
- **contradicted**: a direct check failed or a counterexample was found

Questions to ask:
- Is the claim stated precisely enough to test?
- Is there a runnable or inspectable artifact behind the claim?
- Did the check cover the intended scope?
- Was an attempt made to falsify the claim?
- What risk remains even after the check?
